ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Repair Share is based out of Vancouver, BC. We are situated on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territories of the xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam), səlil̓wətaɁɬ (Tsleil-Waututh), and Sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish) peoples.

Repair Share fosters a greater sense of commnunity through creating a space in which users can lend a hand or ask for help. As a team, we try our best to embody these principles of inclusivity, friendship, and community! Whether it is a cold beer after a hard week of work, or aiding one another with the task at hand, the Repair Share Team is a community onto itself.
